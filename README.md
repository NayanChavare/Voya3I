# **SDG-INSIGHT: A Digital Framework for Assessing SDG Awareness in Higher Education Institutions**

## **1. Introduction**

The **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)** are a global action plan to achieve sustainable progress by 2030. Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) play a major role in spreading awareness, integrating SDGs into academics, and encouraging sustainable campus practices.
However, many institutions **do not have a structured system** to measure how aware students and faculty are about SDGs.

**SDG-INSIGHT** proposes a **digital framework** (web/app-based) to assess, analyze, and report SDG awareness levels in HEIs.

---

## **2. Problem Statement**

Most HEIs lack:

* A standardized tool to assess SDG awareness
* Quantitative measurement of awareness levels
* Role-based insights (students, faculty, staff)
* Analytics dashboards for institutional improvement

---

## **3. Objectives of SDG-INSIGHT**

### Primary Objectives

1. Develop a **digital assessment platform** for SDG awareness.
2. Calculate an **SDG Awareness Score** for individuals and groups.
3. Provide HEI-level reports and analytics dashboards.

### Secondary Objectives

* Identify awareness gaps by department/semester
* Promote improvement through recommendations
* Compare awareness across demographics (gender, year, stream)

## **6. Data Collection Methodology**

### Instruments Used

A structured questionnaire having:

* **Knowledge-Based Questions** (objective)
* **Attitude-Based Questions** (Likert scale)
* **Practice-Based Questions** (behavioral)

Example format:

* MCQ: “How many SDGs exist?”
* Likert: “SDGs should be part of curriculum (Strongly Agree → Strongly Disagree)”
* Practice: “Do you participate in sustainability activities?”

---

## **7. Framework Design (Key Components)**

### **A. SDG Awareness Dimensions**

| Dimension              | Description                            |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Knowledge              | SDG facts, names, goals, purpose       |
| Attitude               | interest and importance perception     |
| Engagement             | participation in SDG activities/events |
| Institutional Exposure | curriculum, seminars, campaigns        |

---

### **B. SDG Awareness Scoring Model**

Each assessment is scored as:

**Total Score = Knowledge Score + Attitude Score + Engagement Score + Exposure Score**

Score categories:

* **0–25%** → Low Awareness
* **26–50%** → Basic Awareness
* **51–75%** → Moderate Awareness
* **76–100%** → High Awareness

You can also compute:

✅ **Institution SDG Index (ISI)**
Average score of all participants.

✅ **Department Awareness Index (DAI)**
Average score per department.
